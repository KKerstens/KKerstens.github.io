Kristiaan H.J. Kerstens

Research Professor

CNRS - LEM (UMR 9221), Lille & IESEG School of Management, Lille

Short Biography

Summary Profile:

  • Director of research (= Research professor) at “Centre National de la Recherche Scientifique” (CNRS) (laboratory “Lille Economics Management” (LEM), UMR 9221).

  • Secondary position: Professor of Economics at “IESEG School of Management” in Lille (France).

  • Academic economist with broad teaching and research experience.

  • Familiarity with quantitative economics and management techniques.

  • Administrative responsibilities for research coordination, library and information technologies.

Complete Curriculum Vitae

  • Main research specialties:

    • Efficiency measurement (benchmarking) of private and public sector organisations;

    • Productivity indices and indicators (Total Factor Productivity),

    • Portfolio optimisation with higher moments; and

    • Public economics.

  • Visiting positions with teaching (past and present):

    • EDHEC, Lille (France): MBA program;

    • Soochow University, Taipei (Taiwan);

    • Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona, Barcelona (Spain): European Doctoral Program;

    • Université Charles-de-Gaulle (Lille 3), Lille (France);

    • Université Louis Pasteur, Strasbourg (France);

    • Université Catholique Saint Jérôme de Douala, Douala (Cameroun);

    • University of Leicester, Leicester (UK).

  • International work experience.

  • Flexibility and great sense for teamwork.
